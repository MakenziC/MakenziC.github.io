What is Cosmetology?

Cosmetology is the study of how to care for hair, skin, and nails, and how to apply beauty treatments to enhance a person's appearance.

Interests

  • Hair styling: Cutting, coloring, and styling hair
  • Nail care: Manicures, pedicures, and nail art
  • Makeup: Applying makeup for special occasions, such as weddings, fashion shows, and TV and film
